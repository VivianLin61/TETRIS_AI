How To Play

  • Move the tetrominoes left and right with the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys
  • Rotate the terominoes with the UP key.
  • Press the DOWN Arrow key to perform a soft drop. (The tetromino will fall faster than normal)
  • Press the SPACE bar to perform a hard drop. (The tetromino will fall straight down)
  • Press the C key to activate the hold queue.
